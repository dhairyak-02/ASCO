Film Rebate Advisory

Film rebate and incentive schemes have become an important financial consideration for film and content producers. At Anil Sekhri & Company, we provide specialised advisory services to assist clients in understanding, structuring, and availing domestic and international film rebate programmes.

Scope of Film Rebate Advisory

Our services are designed to support producers throughout the lifecycle of a project, from planning and budgeting to certification and audit of qualifying expenditure.

  • • Advisory on domestic and international film incentive schemes
  • • Structuring of production budgets for rebate eligibility
  • • Review and certification of qualifying expenditure
  • • Documentation and compliance support
  • • Coordination with relevant authorities and agencies

Industry & Client Relevance

Our film rebate advisory services are relevant for film production houses, OTT content producers, international studios, and independent producers undertaking domestic or overseas productions.

Our Approach

With decades of experience in the Media & Entertainment sector, we adopt a practical and compliance-focused approach to film rebate advisory. Our emphasis is on accurate documentation, transparent reporting, and timely coordination to ensure smooth processing of rebate claims.
