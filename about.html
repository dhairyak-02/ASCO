Our History

Established in 1983

Established in Mumbai in 1983 by Anil Sekhri, Anil Sekhri & Company swiftly carved out its niche in the financial landscape, specializing in Media & Entertainment from its inception. With our proprietary processes and over four decades of experience, our firm has been at the forefront of the industry’s transformation, playing a pivotal role in its evolution into the vibrant stage of the 21st century.

Under Anil Sekhri’s leadership, our team has garnered extensive expertise in providing tailored financial solutions for clients ranging from leading celebrities in film, television, and advertising to performers, authors, and production houses. Our comprehensive services encompass financial advisory, taxation, budgeting, and costing, with a proven track record of success.

Leadership

Under Anil Sekhri’s personal guidance, we have successfully navigated the financial intricacies of numerous movies, ensuring seamless execution and optimal financial outcomes. Moreover, Anil Sekhri’s position as an Independent Director across multiple listed and unlisted companies underscores our firm’s dedication to governance and strategic supervision.

At Anil Sekhri & Company, we pride ourselves on our legacy of excellence and unwavering dedication to our clients’ success. As we continue to navigate the ever-evolving landscape of Media and Entertainment, we remain steadfast in our mission to provide innovative financial solutions that empower our clients to thrive in their endeavors.
