Turnaround & Restructuring

Businesses may encounter financial or operational challenges due to market conditions, liquidity constraints, or structural inefficiencies. At Anil Sekhri & Company, we provide structured turnaround and restructuring advisory services aimed at stabilising operations and restoring long-term viability.

Scope of Turnaround & Restructuring Services

Our services are designed to identify underlying issues and support management in implementing corrective measures through a structured and disciplined approach.

  • • Financial diagnostics and business viability assessment
  • • Cash flow analysis and working capital optimisation
  • • Restructuring of debt and financial obligations
  • • Advisory on cost rationalisation and operational efficiency
  • • Support in negotiations with lenders and stakeholders

Industry & Client Relevance

Our turnaround and restructuring services are relevant for corporates, SMEs, and group entities facing financial stress, liquidity challenges, or operational inefficiencies across various sectors.

Our Approach

We adopt a practical and solution-oriented approach, focusing on realistic assessment, transparent communication, and implementable strategies. Our objective is to assist businesses in regaining financial stability while safeguarding stakeholder interests.
