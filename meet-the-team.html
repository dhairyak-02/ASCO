Anil Sekhri

Group Managing Partner

Leadership

Anil Sekhri founded the firm in 1983 and has over four decades of professional experience in accounting, taxation, and financial advisory. He has been closely associated with the Media & Entertainment industry since the early years of his practice.

Under his leadership, the firm has advised leading individuals and organisations across films, television, advertising, and corporate sectors. His practical approach and deep industry knowledge have been instrumental in navigating complex financial and regulatory matters.

In addition to his professional practice, Anil Sekhri serves as an Independent Director on the boards of multiple listed and unlisted companies, reflecting the firm’s commitment to governance and strategic oversight.
